Algorithms vs. Appraisers

An AVM can't walk through
your front door.

Automated Valuation Models pull from public data — tax records, recent sales, square footage. They can't see the $40K kitchen renovation. They can't assess foundation issues. They don't know the HOA just approved a special assessment.

A licensed appraiser walks the property, adjusts for what algorithms miss, and delivers a defensible opinion of value. That's the difference between a guess and a number you can use.
