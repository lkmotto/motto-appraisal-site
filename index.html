In the Field

Real deals. Real numbers.

Pre-Listing · Frisco TX

Seller saved $31k by getting a pre-listing appraisal

A Frisco homeowner was quoted $625k by their agent based on comps that included a pool-upgraded property. The appraisal came in at $594k on the same methodology — and the home sold at $596k. The gap between agent estimate and appraisal identified two non-contributing upgrades the market wasn't rewarding. The seller priced correctly day one, avoided a renegotiation, and closed on schedule.

$31k Overpricing avoided
12 days Time on market
DSCR / Investor · Garland TX

Investor acquired at asking after appraisal confirmed the number

A DFW investor was under contract on a Garland SFR at $247k for DSCR financing. The lender's AMC had a two-week backlog. We delivered a licensed desktop appraisal in 3 days. Value came in at $251k. Loan closed without delay, DSCR ratio cleared at 1.31. The $4k spread above contract gave the investor leverage on their equity position from day one.

3 days Report delivery
1.31 DSCR at close
